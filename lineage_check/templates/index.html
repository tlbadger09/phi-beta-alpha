What is Lineage Check?

Lineage Check searches digitized 1870 U.S. Federal Census records to verify your direct descent from Foundational Black Americans — descendants of American chattel slavery.

How it works

We match your ancestor's name against handwritten census records using phonetic matching and cross-reference against {{ "{:,}".format(stats.ipums_total) if stats else "3.4 million" }} verified demographic records to produce a confidence-scored lineage chain.
