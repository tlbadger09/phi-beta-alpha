Anchored Backward Walk

Start from a person that living family members can confirm — typically someone who appears in the 1950 census. The engine walks backward decade by decade to 1870, scoring each link and compounding confidence. At 1870, the chain hands off to the Bridge Engine to cross the emancipation wall.

What makes this different from a regular search: Every link in this chain is anchored to a person a living relative can verify. Confidence compounds down — the weakest link caps the trust of everything earlier. The output is a proven descent path, not a tree.
